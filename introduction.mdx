---
title: What is Agno?
sidebarTitle: Introduction
---

Agno is a multi-agent framework that brings you speed, performance and reliability with a delightful developer experience. We provide a framework for building agents, a runtime for running them in production and a powerful UI for managing your agentic system.

Agno Agents come with built-in memory, knowledge and reasoning. They can be orchestrated as autonomous multi-agent teams or deterministic, stateful workflows. To run your agentic system in production, we provide a FastAPI-based runtime called the AgentOS which comes with a beautiful UI that acts as a control plane for your agentic system.

For organizations building agentic systems, Agno provides a complete solution. You get the most advanced framework for building your agentic system, an API for production and a control plane for managing your production system. Your also get complete data privacy and control, the AgentOS runs on your infrastructure and no data ever leaves your system.
